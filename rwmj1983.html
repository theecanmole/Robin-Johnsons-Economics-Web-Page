MACRO TRENDS IN NEW ZEALAND AGRICULTURE

Introductory Note

During 1983 the Economics Division will be publishing a series of Discussion Papers incorporating commonly used data series for the agricultural sector. The readers attention is drawn to the different definitions used in some of the series, particularly those based on the old national income series and the old gross farm income series as compared with the New Zealand System of National Accounts (SNA) at present in use. Previous papers are The Formulation of a Consistent Gross Farm Income Series : 1950-81, Discussion Paper 2/83; and Capital in New Zealand Agriculture and the Primary Products Processing Sector : 1950-1980, Discussion Paper 3/83
